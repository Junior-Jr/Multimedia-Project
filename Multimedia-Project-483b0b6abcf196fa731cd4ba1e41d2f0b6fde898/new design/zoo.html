Chiang Mai Zoo

  • 100 Huaykaew Rd, Tambon Su Thep, Amphoe Mueang Chiang Mai, Chang Wat Chiang Mai 50200

  • 053 210 374

  • 08:00 - 17:00

Chiang Mai Zoo in Thailand is home to a wide variety of majestic and fascinating wildlife, including rarer species like the panda. A few of the amazing animals here are the koala, meerkat, zebra, and penguin. There is an aquarium too. Although not the first thing that comes to mind when most people think of the city, the Chiang Mai Zoo is surprisingly interesting. Covering 200 acres of land in the foothills of Doi Suthep and providing excellent views of the city in addition to its fascinating wildlife exhibits, Chiang Mai Zoo is certainly worth a visit, especially if you've had your fill of temples.
